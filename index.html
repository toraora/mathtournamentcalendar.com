https://artofproblemsolving.com/community/c5h3086665_25th_elmo_2023_signups The ELMO is an olympiad test similar to both the USAMO and IMO in format. However, because there are only finitely many people who can attend MOP each year, for many years the competition has also been posted and run on AoPS.
